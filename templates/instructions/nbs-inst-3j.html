Instructions

After every letter that you see, you will need to make a decision by pressing either the "F" key or the "J" key.
The following level is called 3-back.
Whenever the current letter is THE SAME as the one presented 3 letters back in the sequence, press the "J" key.
In ALL OTHER CASES, press the "F" key.
